# Use Cases

Perspective AI transforms how you gather insights by conducting natural, AI-powered interviews with your target audience. Whether you're researching customers, validating product decisions, or optimizing business processes, these comprehensive guides show you exactly how to design, conduct, and analyze interviews that drive meaningful results.

Each guide follows our proven 7-step methodology: Define your research question, refine your interview plan, customize the participant experience, invite your audience, let AI conduct the interviews, analyze insights, and implement improvements. Real-world examples and sample analysis questions help you get started immediately.

Choose your use case below to get started:
